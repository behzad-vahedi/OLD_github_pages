ABOUT ME

Behzad Vahedi is a GIScientist with engineering background and experience in GIS development. He is a PhD student in GIScience at University of California Santa Barbara. As a graduate student researcher, he work in Center for Spatial Studies at UCSB. Behzad holds a bachelor degree in Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering, and a Master of Science degree in GIS engineering, both from K.N.Toosi University of Technology, Tehran, Iran.

At UCSB Geography, he studies how to ask and answer spatio-temporal questions under supervision of Professor Werner Kuhn, and his goal is to facilitate Question-Based Spqtial Computing. His focus is on Field as a Core Concept of Spatial Information and he develops Core Computations on fields by extending Map Algebra operations from rastre to field. He is always passionate to develop GIS services and software, provide GIS solutions, and to work on challenging and real world spatial problems.

If you want to know more about the projects he has done, check his Github, and feel free to send him an email if you want to contact.
